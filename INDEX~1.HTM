Price Tracker & Cashback for Amazon

A free Chrome extension that quietly tracks Amazon prices as you browse, so you always know if today's price is actually a good deal — and lets you earn cashback when you buy.

What it does

📈

Automatic price tracking

Browse Amazon like normal — prices are logged locally as you visit product pages.

🔔

Price drop alerts

Get notified when a product you're tracking drops 5% or more.

💰

Cashback on purchases

Click "Shop with Cashback" to buy through a Rakuten affiliate link and earn money back.

🔒

100% local & private

All price history and settings stay on your device. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

How it works

  1. Install the extensionAvailable free on the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Browse Amazon as usualEvery product page you visit gets logged to your local price history — no extra clicks.
  3. Check the popup any timeSee the current price, 90-day high/low, and how much you'd save.
  4. Buy through "Shop with Cashback"Opens your Rakuten affiliate link — only when you click it, never automatically.

Price Tracker & Cashback participates in the Rakuten Advertising affiliate program. When you click "Shop with Cashback" and complete a qualifying purchase, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. This only ever happens as the direct result of your own click on that button.
