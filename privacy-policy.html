Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

Price Tracker & Cashback for Amazon ("the extension") is built to work entirely on your device. This policy explains exactly what data we handle and what we don't.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not collect or transmit your personal information.
  • We do not track your browsing activity outside of Amazon product pages.
  • We do not send any data to our own servers — we don't operate any servers that receive your data.
  • We do not store passwords, payment details, or Amazon account information.
  • We do not sell or share data with third parties.

What we DO store, and where

  • Locally, in Chrome's built-in storage on your device only: product titles, images, URLs, and price history for Amazon product pages you visit while tracking is enabled.
  • Locally: your extension settings (tracking on/off, notification preferences, and your own Rakuten affiliate link template, if you choose to add one).

None of this data ever leaves your browser. Uninstalling the extension deletes it permanently. You can also clear it at any time from the extension's Settings page.

Affiliate links and cashback

When you click the "Shop with Cashback" button, the extension opens a Rakuten affiliate link for the product you're viewing, in a new tab. This only happens as a direct result of that click — never automatically or in the background. Rakuten's own privacy policy governs what happens after you land on their site. We may earn a commission on qualifying purchases made through that link, at no extra cost to you.

Permissions we request, and why

  • storage — to save price history and settings locally.
  • notifications — to alert you about price drops, if enabled.
  • alarms — reserved for scheduling housekeeping tasks (e.g. trimming old history).
  • Host permission for Amazon domains — to read product price/title/image on pages you visit, so we can log them.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, note it in the extension's release notes.
